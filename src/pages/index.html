Our Ethos

Shapeland is the world's first theme park dedicated solely to shape-recognition in the early years. Experts agree that shape-recognition is an important pre-cursor to later literacy and numeracy skills. Enter our magical world and your little ones will earn all about geometric shapes through fun and engaging rides and activities! Even our food and drink is shape-themed! But most importantly, our family-focused facilities are world-renowned with baby pit stop stations in accessible locations for feeding and changing, quiet areas for naps, drinking water fountains across the park, and a range of healthy snack stations at every turn.
