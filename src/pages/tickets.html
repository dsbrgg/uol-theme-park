Payment Options

Visit our dedicated box office team at the park gates for limited edition shape-themed tickets & lanyards. Our box office accepts cards & cash.

Alternatively, visit any one of our ticket kiosks at the park gates to purchase our standard tickets. Card payment only.

You can also download our Shapeland app on Apple or Android to purchase e-tickets. Card Payment Only.

We accept all major credit & debit cards.
